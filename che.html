Documentary Film Project: WRITERS

Primary Location: Narathiwat, Thailand

EDITOR: The Melayu Review

Zakariya Amataya won the Southeast Asia Writers Award (or, SEAWrite) in 2010 for the trilingual poetry collection No Woman in Poetry (2010) and went to Hawaii as an artist-in-residence at the Doris Duke Shangri La Museum of Art, Culture and Design. In Hawaii, I started to document his journey and the mutual love for the sea that inspired his second collection, If as Deep as the Ocean Within Us (2013). When he finally returned home to the southern border region in 2015, after living in Thailand's capital city for over two decades, I followed with my camera. At work with his third poetry collection, he manages a literary and intellectual yearbook from the region called The Melayu Review. As a collaborative pursuit, Che partners with community organizations to organize writing workshops and grassroots media channels to help rebuild a space that has, since the imposition of martial law in 2004, experienced significant restrictions on political speech. The documentary is about the challenges and possibilities of his pursuit.
