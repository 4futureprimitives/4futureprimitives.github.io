RESONANT BODIES

ABOUT   WRITERS   LECTURES   MUSIC  

The Thai scholar and former president of the Assoociation for Asian Studies, Thongchai Winichakul, remarked that reading literacy is the way out of a political deadlock. This emphasis on reading runs through a range of scholars, for instance, Julia Kristeva's emphasis on how writers read through and break the conventions of language (geno-text) which inspires Roland Barthes' emphasis on reading music (geno-song). Building from these contributions, I investigate how to read voice from a variety of materials including maps, cinema, and the vinyl LP.
