FUTURE PRIMITIVE

A film about free speech

Ten years ago, the Malay Muslim poet Zakariya Amataya won the Southeast Asia Writers Award. The event coincided with a tremendous surge of violence in his home region, which today remains under the grip of Southeast Asia's largest military occupation. From the long traditions of local narrative to the catastrophic impact of political events, our project addresses the voyages of poetry in motion.
